JHU Office of Sustainability
I worked as the communications person (full time when I took off a semester from school and as an intern when I went back to school). There I contributed graphic design, copy writing, news gathering, and print and social media marketing and communications. I created marketing tools, branding packages, and promotional resources for university-wide campaigns and sustainability awareness. I spent some time of my time there redesigning their website (if only we still had an example of what we had to work with still living online), and it was mostly on a consulting/strategy/content generation role. We had to consult with the web development team out of Hopkins, but most of the parts that I came up with are still at sustainability.jhu.edu. My design sample from my time here is the logo I designed for JHU's Earth Week 2012. This logo appeared on all promotional materials across every JHU campus.
